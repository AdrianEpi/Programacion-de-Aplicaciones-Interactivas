generateEquation() → {object}

Generates the parabolic equation that follows the projectile where (Vx, Vy) are the coordinates of the vertex of the parabola, and (x, y) the coordinates of one point of the parabola. As we are shooting fom the x and y axis the point (0, 0) will always be part of our parabolic function so we use it as default.. (x - Vy)^2 = c(y - Vx) c = ((x - Vy)^2) / (y - Vx) y = (((x - Vy)^2) / c) + Vx
